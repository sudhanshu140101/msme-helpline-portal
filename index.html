How MSME Helpline Works

From profile creation to funding readiness in 5 simple steps

1

Create Business Profile

Register your business with basic information including company name, PAN, GST, and industry type. Verify your ownership with a simple OTP authentication.

📋 Required: Business Name, PAN, Mobile OTP, Email
2

Upload Documents Securely

Share your GST certificates, bank statements (last 12 months), balance sheets, and other compliance documents. All data is encrypted end-to-end.

📁 Supported: PDF, Excel, Images (256-bit SSL encrypted)
3

Report Payment

Make payment to get Due Diligence Report

✓ Real-time: PAN, GST, MCA, Bank Verification
4

API Data Verification

Our system verifies your data against government and banking databases through integrated APIs. Automatic verification of PAN, GST compliance, and business registration.

✓ Real-time: PAN, GST, MCA, Bank Verification
5

AI Due Diligence Report

Advanced analytics generate a comprehensive due diligence report including financial profile, cash flow analysis, compliance status.

📊 Report Includes: Financial Score, Risk Assessment, Trends Analysis
